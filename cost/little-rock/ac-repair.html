Even within the Little Rock-North Little Rock-Conway metro area, you will find a wide range of prices. Several factors drive this variation.

National chains like Midas, Pep Boys, and Firestone fall somewhere in between. For most routine automotive AC diagnosis, repair, and recharge, an independent shop with strong reviews offers the best value.

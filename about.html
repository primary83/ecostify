About

We believe you should know what things cost before you get the bill.

Ecostify is an AI-powered tool that gives you fair cost estimates for any auto or home service — repairs, upgrades, remodeling, detailing, and everything in between. No accounts, no fees, no upsells. Just honest pricing information.

The Problem

Every year, millions of homeowners and car owners overpay for services simply because they don't know what things should cost. A car wrap quoted at $5,000 that should be $3,000. A kitchen remodel estimated at $40K when the real range is $25K. A detailing package at $800 when the market rate is $400. The information gap between you and the person writing the estimate is where overcharging lives.

Our Solution

Ecostify closes that gap. Snap a photo of your car or home — or describe what you need — and our AI instantly tells you what it typically costs, what to watch out for, and what questions to ask. Then we connect you with top-rated local providers who can actually do the work.

Whether it's a brake repair, full vehicle wrap, bathroom remodel, or pool maintenance — we don't replace professionals. We arm you with the knowledge to work with them as an informed customer.

Transparency First

We show you real price ranges based on national and regional data. No hidden agendas, no paid placements in our estimates.

Always Free

Getting a cost estimate should never cost money. Ecostify is free to use, no account required, no strings attached.

Privacy Respected

We don't store your photos or personal data. Your uploaded images are analyzed in real-time and never saved to our servers.

Local Focus

Our provider recommendations prioritize local businesses with strong reputations and verified reviews from real customers.

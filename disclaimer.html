AI Limitations

Our AI analysis has inherent limitations:

Factors That Affect Actual Costs

Actual repair costs can differ significantly from our estimates due to:

  • Geographic location and local labor rates

  • Quality and brand of replacement parts used
  • Discovery of additional issues during repair
  • Emergency or after-hours service fees
  • Permits, inspections, or code compliance requirements
  • Current supply chain conditions affecting part availability
  • The experience level and specialization of the provider
